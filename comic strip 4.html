Good vs Bad Prompts in Generative AI

User: Write a story.
AI: Sure, here's a story: Once upon a time, there was a character...
User: Write a 200-word story about a dragon who learns to trust humans, focusing on emotions and character development.
AI: Once upon a time, a fierce dragon named Drakron lived alone in the mountains. His heart was full of anger and distrust...

Why is this a bad prompt?

This prompt is too vague! Simply asking to "write a story" doesn't provide enough direction for the AI to create a meaningful or interesting response. Specific details about the type of story, the characters, or the setting help generate better results.

Why is this a better prompt?

This prompt is clear and specific! It provides a clear character (the dragon), a theme (trusting humans), and a focus on emotions and character development. This gives the AI enough context to create a much richer and more engaging story.
