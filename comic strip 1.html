1. Artificial Intelligence (AI): A field of computer science that enables machines to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as learning, problem-solving, and decision-making. 1
2. Generative AI: A subset of AI that uses models like neural networks to generate new content such as text, images, and music, based on patterns learned from existing data. 2
3. Machine Learning: A branch of AI where algorithms learn and improve from data without being explicitly programmed for every task. 3
